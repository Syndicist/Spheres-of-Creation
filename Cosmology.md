# The Beginning
In the beginning, there were the Spheres. Each Sphere was pure, fundamental, and eternal. They shaped worlds around themselves without reason or intention, filled with beings of like purity, the Wyrms. Then, something happened. One of these Spheres moved.

The Sphere would move through the empty space between the Sphers, leaving a trail of its essence in its wake until, inevitably, it collided with another Sphere in an event oh so cosmically significant and unprecedented. The result? The first sphere ever created, the first Collapse, impure, composite.

One collision lead to another, and another again until the Spheres were crashing into eachother constantly. Chaos overtook the whole of the universe.

The pure beings of the Spheres, the Wyrms, saw this disorder and came to a simultaneous realization: they must bring order to their beloved Spheres. The Wyrms from each original sphere, understanding the power of Collapse, of Compositing. They Collapsed themselves into the Great Wyrms, still somewhat pure in their ways, yet still composite. With their combined power, they then Collapsed the whole of the universe in one simultaneous, harmonious motion, stilling the chaos into one condensed plane: The Great Plane. The Spheres, original and new, finally stilled, were plucked from the Great Plane by the Great Wyrms and set across the cosmos to their appropriate positions. However, what was collapsed could not be expanded. The Great Wyrms could not enjoy their home Spheres, having outgrown their cradles. They would have succumb to complete despair, had not one of them spotted a curiosity.

All around the Great Plane, new beings arose. Impure. Composite. Born Collapsed. The Great Wyrms took interest in these new kindred souls, but lacked the finesse to engage with them on equal terms. So, they took to their favorites, focusing their energy in communicating with the composites most-alike. They merely sought companionship, but instead found worshippers: a confounding result indeed.

Eons pass. The impure beings, children of the Collapse, live their lives as they see fit to the continued fascination of the Great Wyrms.

However... What were to happen should the Great Wyrms grow tired and weary, and the Spheres move untethered again...?
